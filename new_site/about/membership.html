By joining the Friendship Sloop Society, you become part of a community dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich maritime heritage of the Friendship sloop. We look forward to welcoming you aboard!

All Memberships Receive:

  • All Society Mailings
  • The Annual Yearbook
  • A Membership Card
  • One Membership Decal
  • Entry Rights for Participation in the Society Regattas and Functions
  • Voting Rights for Two Adults

The Friendship Sloop Society offers several types of memberships to accommodate different levels of involvement and interest:

  • Individual Membership: Ideal for individuals who own a Friendship sloop or have a keen interest in these historic vessels. Members receive newsletters, event invitations, and access to Society resources.
  • Family Membership: Suitable for families who wish to participate in Society activities together. Includes all benefits of individual membership for all family members.
  • Associate Membership: Designed for those who appreciate the Friendship sloop but do not own one. Members receive newsletters and event invitations.
  • Corporate Membership: For businesses and organizations that support the Society's mission. Includes promotional opportunities and recognition at events.
  • Lifetime Membership: A one-time payment option for individuals or families who wish to support the Society long-term. Includes all benefits of individual or family membership for life.
