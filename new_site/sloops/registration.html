New Sloop Registration

In order for the authentication committee to determine whether or not your sloop is eligible, please provide as much information as available! As a minimum, PLEASE PROVIDE PHOTOGRAPHS; particularly photographs of the hull which illustrate the bottom shape and photographs while under sail. If your sloop was built from customer designed plans, it is requested that a copy of the plans be provided for the Society archival collection.

Upon receipt of this request, the Authentication Committee, as per Article VI.c of the FSS Constitution shall rule on the eligibility of sloop owners applying for membership in the Society. Two classes shall be established and defined as follows:

  • Class "A" Originals: Bonafide Friendship Sloops built before 1920 and approved by the authentication committee
  • Class "B" True Replicas: Sloops built after 1920 and approved by the authentication committee

Upon approval, the owner will be issued a sail number and a certificate of authenticity. The sail number shall be permanently affixed to the sloop in a location visible from the waterline. The sail number shall be in the form of "XXX" where "XXX" is the number assigned by the Society.
