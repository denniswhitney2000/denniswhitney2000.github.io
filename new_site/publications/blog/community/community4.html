Rockland's Festival Celebrates Friendship Sloops and Maritime Heritage

News Center Maine Features Friendship Sloop Festival

Rockland, Maine - The Friendship Sloop Society's annual festival was recently featured on News Center Maine. The festival, which celebrates the rich maritime heritage of Rockland and the iconic Friendship Sloops, drew a large crowd of sailing enthusiasts and spectators.

This NewsCenter Maine video highlights Rockland's new maritime festival, celebrating 100-year-old Friendship sloops. These single-mast, gaff-rigged boats, originally used for fishing, now symbolize Maine's rich maritime heritage. The festival features historic sloop races, uniting enthusiasts who preserve their craftsmanship and tradition. Owners cherish these vessels for their timeless beauty and legacy
