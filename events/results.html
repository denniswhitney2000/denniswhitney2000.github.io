Homecoming Rendezvous

The Friendship Sloop Homecoming Regatta in Rocland ME is an annual sailing event celebrating this traditional fishing vessel originating from Friendship, Maine. These elegant wooden, fiberglass and steel boats, known for their distinctive design and seaworthiness, have been a symbol of Maine's maritime heritage since the late 1800s. The regatta brings together sloop owners, sailors, and enthusiasts to honor this iconic vessel. Participants compete in races, showcase their beautifully restored boats and share stories of the sloop's rich history. The event fosters a sense of community and preserves the legacy of the Friendship Sloop for future generations.
