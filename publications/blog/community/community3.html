Former Treasurer Rev. David Bell has passed away at 92

David W. Bell, 92, of Kennebunk, passed away on Oct. 1, 2024. A dedicated Methodist minister for 38 years, he passionately served small community churches in Maine and New Hampshire, fostering ecumenical events and supporting single adults. An Eagle Scout and avid sailor, he also served as treasurer of the Friendship Sloop Society. In retirement, he continued ministry work, built a home on Pemaquid Point with his wife Nancy Jane, and later partnered with Nancy Ellen Treadwell. Survived by children, grandchildren, and loved ones, his life will be celebrated on Nov. 16. Donations in his memory may be made to The Preachers' Aid Society or The Church on the Cape.
