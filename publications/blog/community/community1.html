Sailing Action at the 2016 Homecoming at Rockland

The 2016 Homecoming at Rockland was a spectacular event filled with thrilling sailing action. Enthusiasts from all over gathered to witness the majestic Friendship Sloops in action. The event showcased the rich maritime heritage of Rockland and provided an opportunity for sailors and spectators to come together and celebrate their love for sailing.
